About Us

Do It Greener Foundation was the brainchild of Yvette Brown and her daughter, Ayden. For several years, Yvette and Ayden researched how making small and simple changes can make a positive impact on our environment. The smartest way to ensure that this happens is to get the community to work together as a team. As a result, Yvette and Ayden decided to found DIG as a nonprofit organization dedicated to making our community, our state, our country, as well as our world, more eco-friendly. The Do It Greener (DIG) Foundation was established in 2020 as a 501(c)(3) organization dedicated to sustainably enhancing quality of life and education technology opportunities. DIG’s organizational structure ensures its objectives are comprehensive in scope and inherently inclusive by maintaining a diverse board of professionals from key areas of insight.

Our initiatives include sustainable practices regarding period poverty, beautification of our communities, health and wellness, and workforce development for underrepresented populations.

We truly believe that as a community striving for sustainability, innovation, and inclusivity, we can all do things a little “greener.”
